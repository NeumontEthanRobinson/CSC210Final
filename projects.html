Number Guessing Game

I made a working number guessing game that allows the player to chose from 4 difficulties: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Custom. Each difficulty controls the range of numbers and the amount of guesses allowed. The custom difficulty allows the player to choose those values for themselves.

Text Based RPG

I made a RPG that uses java to make a text based battle system and enviroment. It includes a class creation system with 6 classes for the player. It includes a wide variety of weapons and armor for the player to use. You can battle through 15 different enemies that are selected use a weighted rng system. Each encounter ends in a reward of either weapons or armor.

The Midnight Chronicles, Case #1: The Reels of Death

This is a work in progress project based on a friend's D&D system and campaign. In this game you will go through Las Vegas whilst trying to solve a mystery involving a mysterious creature that the most people can remember is the sound of a roulette wheel clicking. Escape from this creature and working with a benefactor to eventually take it down.
